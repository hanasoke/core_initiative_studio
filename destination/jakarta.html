About Tourism

Jakarta, officially named the Special Capital Region of Jakarta or DKI Jakarta, is the capital city of Indonesia and also an autonomous province-level region. Jakarta has five administrative cities and one administrative regency. In a general sense, Jakarta is considered a metropolitan city. It is located on the northwest coast of Java Island. Formerly known as Sunda Kelapa, Jayakarta, and Batavia, Jakarta is also nicknamed "The Big Durian" as it is regarded as a city equivalent to New York City (Big Apple).
